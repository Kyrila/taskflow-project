About Us

Music App is a space created for music lovers of all genres. Our goal is to offer a simple, visual and inspiring way to discover and enjoy music.

From Rap to Classical, from Indie to Flamenco, we believe that every style has a unique story to tell. This app is a tribute to that diversity and to all the artists behind it.

This project has been designed as part of a learning journey, combining design, usability and front-end development.
